At Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Laboratory (APL) in Maryland, Jennifer Sleeman and Marisa Hughes are using AI to model and forecast air quality.

Jennifer Sleeman from APL said:
"We’re speeding things up because we’re asking the models to compute shorter timesteps, which is easier and faster to do, and we’re using the deep-learning emulator to simulate those ensembles and account for variations in weather data."
