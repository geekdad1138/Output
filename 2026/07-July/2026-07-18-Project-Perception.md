Microsoft's Project Perception is a new cybersecurity tool that orchestrates requests across Anthropic, OpenAI or its own models to address security vulnerabilities with AI.

The big difference being that this tool could offer remediation for vulnerabilities at a much lower cost, as they can route different tasks to different models

Reports are that this new product could come out as soon as this month, spearheaded by Microsoft's new head of security Hayete Gallot.

From Firstpost:
"Since taking charge in February, Gallot has reportedly reorganised Microsoft’s security business to place greater emphasis on AI-powered products while reducing investment in older offerings. The changes are aimed at reinforcing Microsoft’s leadership in enterprise security as competition intensifies."
