At the University of Minnesota, they've been using aerial robots with AI to analyze wildfires.

The team of Professor Jiarong Hong, along with Nikil Krishnakumar, Shashank Sharma and Srijan Kumar Pal helped develop the technology.

Nikil Krishnakumar said:
"This approach allows for high-resolution data collection across large areas—at a lower cost than satellite-based tools,"
