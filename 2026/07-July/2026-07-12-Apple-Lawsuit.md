Apple is getting their lawyers rounded up and going after OpenAI, claiming that they are luring Apple employees away so they can steal confidential trade secrets.

Apparently OpenAI has hired over 400 former Apple employees, and they are claiming that they are encouraging Apple employees to bring confidential information and even hardware with them when they leave.

It’s an interesting lawsuit, because the AI world has seen a lot of people jumping companies with lucrative offers from competitors.
