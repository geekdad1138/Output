Apple is getting their lawyers rounded up and going after OpenAI, claiming that they are luring Apple employees away so they can steal confidential trade secrets.

Apparently OpenAI has hired over 400 former Apple employees, and they are claiming that they are encouraging Apple employees to bring confidential information and even hardware with them when they leave.

It’s an interesting lawsuit, because the AI world has seen a lot of people jumping companies with lucrative offers from competitors.

When OpenAI fired Sam Altman and Greg Brockman, Microsoft walked in with job offers for 700 employees.
https://www.windowscentral.com/microsoft/microsoft-hires-openais-sam-altman-after-being-fired-as-ceo-last-week

Anthropic split from OpenAI when Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei left and took top talent with them.
https://finance.yahoo.com/news/anthropic-ceo-says-why-quit-194409797.html

Meta and Mark Zuckerburg came out with multi-million dollar job offers for AI experts at Google, OpenAI or anywhere else with the right experts.
https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/jun/28/mark-zuckerberg-ai-list

Microsoft hired away pretty much all of Inflection’s staff including Mustafa Suleyman

Elon Musk took talent from Tesla and hired them at xAI, and sued OpenAI for stealing talent.
