In Alberta, the company AltaLink has been working on wildfire detection cameras using AI to automatically scan feeds for signs of wildfire and send alerts.

Scott Schifando from ALERTWest said:
"images are fed into an AI model trained using images of known wildfires. If the system identifies a possible ignition, ALERTWest staff review the detection before an alert is sent to utilities and fire agencies."
