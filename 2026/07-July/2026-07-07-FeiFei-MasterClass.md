Just finished watching [Fei-Fei Li](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fei-fei-li-4541247/)’s incredible MasterClass “AI’s Future - and Yours”. It’s not often in life that you get to actually see and hear from the pillars of the technological advancement in history.

Probably the most impressive thing about Fei-Fei is that she didn’t seek to turn her early advancements in AI into something self serving, or for her own financial gain.

She founded the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered AI (HAI), and seeks to educate the world on how to use AI for good.

From her MasterClass: “In order for Al to fulfill the dreams and hopes of the society and the future we want to live in, as a society, let's try our best to turn Al to the great use, to improve our way of life, to make our health care system better, make governments more efficient, make manufacturing more efficient, make our environment better.”

It’s a wonderful perspective on using AI for good, and I hope we can all point our compasses in that direction.
