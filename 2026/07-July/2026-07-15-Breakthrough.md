My marker board quote for the week.
"The biggest breakthroughs
in AI start with
the best questions"

It’s so tempting with AI to just start pair programming with it, or just throw out a quick prompt and let it run.

But whenever I stop and think for a minute, and form a bigger better question.. it’s amazing the breakthroughs I see.

Sometimes the best question to AI is.. here’s my environment.. got any good ideas?
