My marker board quote for the week.
"AI
turns curiosity
into momentum"

 When you’ve been employed as a developer for as long as I have, it’s easy to just focus on momentum.. whatever moves the needle.

But curiosity, innovation, inspiration.. those are the things that change the game, fix decades old broken processes, and provide that huge boost to your momentum that keeps you going.

Just recently I was feeling the familiar pressure of mounting support tickets and the struggle of trying to standardize our CI/CD environment with mixed tech stacks.

I sat down with my AI assistant, came up with a way to close out 30+ tickets, and rewrote the entire CI/CD automation process. All while still working on other projects.

Stay curious, and bring your AI with you for the ride.
