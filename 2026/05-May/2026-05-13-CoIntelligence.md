Just finished “Co-Intelligence: Living and Working with AI” by Ethan Mollick. Wow. This is one of those books that had me listening to the audio book, pointing at the screen shouting “YES! YES! Exactly!” Or just slack jawed amazed at the insight, understanding and experience from his history with AI.

I think my favorite part is learning that as an educator he pushes his students to understand and use AI, instead of banning it or discouraging it.

It’s given me a lot to think about, from centaurs, to cyborgs, to aliens. It’s a must read.
