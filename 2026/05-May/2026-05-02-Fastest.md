My AI marker board quote for the week.

"AI is the fastest
way to find out
if an idea actually works"

The barrier to try something new is so low now. I’ve taken entire code bases, told copilot to completely rewrite on another technology and walked away. Come back later and review.. good idea or bad idea?

In the past I wouldn’t even try that, no time to play with new ideas, there’s work to be done.

But now I can just throw out a whole project to be done in parallel while I’m working on other things, and sometimes discover incredible ways to change the way we work here. Or sometimes discover things we won’t do!

The fun toys are no longer out of reach!
