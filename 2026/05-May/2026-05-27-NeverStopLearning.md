"The best AI results
come from people who
never stop learning."

My marker board quote for the week. I think this one hits really strong on multiple points.
First is that the best AI results come from people. Those really big wins, game changers, transformational uses for AI come from people dreaming big and using AI to execute.
Second is the lesson we all need to hold in our hearts, to never stop learning. That applies to more than just AI, but with the way things are changing so fast every day if you aren’t open to learning every day, you will miss out on some amazing developments.

Combine it all together and put simply:
People + Learning + AI = The Best
