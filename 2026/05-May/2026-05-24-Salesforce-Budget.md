Salesforce just announced they're planning to spend $300 million on tokens with Anthropic in 2026. They put a freeze on hiring software engineers in 2025, and instead have diverted that cash to the AI integration used by their remaining 15,000 engineers.

They're increasing headcount by the thousands in sales departments to try and explain and promote the new AI developments.

When software development goes from coding assistants to abstracted agents doing all the code without ever even showing you an IDE, it spikes token usage by 10X or more.

So companies that change from software developers using AI paired programmers to write code, to supervisors overseeing agents will see a huge increase in their bill for AI tokens. Replacing human developers with AI comes with a big price tag.

From the article, salesforce's CEO [Marc Benioff](https://www.linkedin.com/in/marcbenioff/) says:
"When they start to use these models, they're now working not only with the AI, but agents to help them code – and they can even become somewhat supervisory over these agents. But still, those engineers are needed. The model still cannot operate autonomously,"
