The Microsoft Ability Summit was a fantastic presentation, showing the ways AI is being used to help make advancements in accessibility for all users.

In a time when a lot of focus is on tokens, stock prices, and ROI, it's truly refreshing to see some focus directed toward inclusion.

I really liked one of the comments a speaker made about taking into account accessibility for software development:
"If you design for the edges, you get the middle for free."
