GPT 5.5 is bringing AI to your computer as more than just a chat or coding assist. It’s moving on to interact with apps like spreadsheets, presentations and browsers.

Should be really interesting to see how people adapt to using it as a full computer assistant not just a chat interface. Between OpenClaw, Copilot for office, Manus My Computer and Anthropic’s Computer Use, the race is on for control of the desktop. A big next step for that to happen is trusting the AI, and AI companies with that next level of access to your information.

From the article OpenAI President Greg Brockman says:
“it’s much better at creating slides, spreadsheets, much better at computer use, using your browser, being able to kind of click through applications that are otherwise hard to have an AI operate”
