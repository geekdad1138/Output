Today Microsoft is releasing their 365 E7 Frontier Suite, packaging their upscaled version or Copilot and Agents in an easy package for purchasing.

Many of the AI tools they’ve built have been add ons requiring a separate purchases. With this move they’re trying to make AI tools the standard package, not something only a select few have.

Microsoft continues to lean into the “Frontier Firm” concept, pushing for companies to adopt AI transformations following their playbook.

In the article Nicole Dezen writes:
“The first wave of AI was about efficiency, doing the same work faster. The Frontier era is about something bigger: redesigning how intelligence shows up inside work to unlock creativity, innovation, and growth. The real question customers face now is how quickly they are ready to move from experimentation to execution.”
