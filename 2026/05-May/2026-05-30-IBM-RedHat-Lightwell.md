IBM and Red Hat’s Project Lightwell - a 5 billion dollar initiative to try and fight back against the growing threat of attackers using AI to exploit open source software vulnerabilities.

They’re building out a subscription service that can use AI to validate exploits, generate fixes, and remediate them all at the speed of AI.

With the recent vulnerabilities discovered in open source code by Opus and the upcoming Mythos, the ability to review and act on exploits urgently and quickly is key to keep confidence high in the safety and security of OSS.

From the article:
“Open source is the backbone of today’s digital economy and the foundation of modern AI, and we are at an inflection point in how it is built, secured, and scaled,” said [Arvind Krishna](https://www.linkedin.com/in/arvindkrishna/), IBM’s Chairman and CEO
