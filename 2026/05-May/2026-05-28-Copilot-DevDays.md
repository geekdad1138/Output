Amazing stuff today at GitHub Copilot DevDays. I love these sessions, because they really dive in quickly, give short easy to consume demos, show off new and cool things on the leading edge.

Things in today’s video: showing off the “awesome-copilot” tools, some good demos using the CLI with lots of different features, agent-first development in copilot, setting up BYOK (even local tools like ollama), using agents in full Visual Studio and enabling fleet mode for agents.

Every time I tune in to these demos it’s like a “todo” list for me to go play around and try to copy what they did, usually bringing my development up another level.
