In Uber’s aggressive adoption of AI, they ran up an expensive bill with token usage, and used up their entire 2026 token budget in April.

It can be difficult to find the right balance to push for corporate AI adoption at the right cost.
Some companies go broke with tokenmaxxing. Others successfully transform their organizations and future proof their company.

From the article, Uber COO [Andrew Macdonald](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrewmacdonald6/) said:
“If you’re not actually able to draw a direct line to how [many] useful features and functionality you’re shipping to your users, that trade becomes harder to justify,”

AI isn’t a technology companies can afford to ignore. But they also can’t stop hiring, replace everyone with AI and expect the magic to happen for free.
