esterday Apple came out to Michigan State University to host their Apple Manufacturing Academy’s Spring Forum.

The forum hosted hundreds of US manufacturers to talk about the role AI can play on the manufacturing floor. From physical AI to smart manufacturing, to implementing full scale AI corporate transformations, Apple held discussions with companies, students, and even had a panel discussion between Apple’s Priya Balasubramaniam and Kevin Guskiewicz, Michigan State University’s president.

Balasubramaniam said:
“We created the Apple Manufacturing Academy with Michigan State because we wanted to bring advanced manufacturing techniques to American manufacturers,”

Apple has committed to invest $600 billion over the next four years in American businesses, and this forum was part of their Apple Manufacturing Program (AMP)
