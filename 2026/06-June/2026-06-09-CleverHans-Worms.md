Self replicating worms using local AI models. This is not the title of a new horror film but maybe it should be?

[Nicolas Papernot](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nicolaspapernot/) from the CleverHans lab collaborated with University of Toronto, Vector Institute, University of Cambridge, and ServiceNow to make this scary worm.

In the world where Mythos/Fable was just released, this is more relevant than ever. It feels like we’re moving into the realm of AI versus AI battles to protect our infrastructure, in a constantly changing race to cover every vulnerability.

This work that CleverHans launched even went out and read up on recent CVEs released after its initial build and used those to successfully hack into 23 of the 33 machines on the test environment.

One of the things that has been holding back these highly capable new models from attacking systems is the cost of tokens as well as the hosts ability to revoke API Keys. But with this fully contained local AI model, that is no deterrent.

Incredibly powerful amazing stuff, but also pretty darn scary.
