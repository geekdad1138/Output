Do AIs dream of electric sheep? OpenAI just launched V3 of their “Dreaming” for ChatGPT.

In previous versions, users were able to tell ChatGPT to store things in memory, or delete existing memories. And the system could use the context from the open chat window or even project files.

Dreaming is a little different. In the words of OpenAI, dreaming can:
“automatically curate memories in the background by referencing chat history”

Similar to how human brains organize and store important memories from the day into long term memory while we sleep and dream.
