"AI Transformation
Begins with trust
not technology"

My marker board quote for the week. I feel like this one goes beyond AI. In the workplace, in life, family, friendships.. trust is such a core piece of what makes our relationships and interactions strong and reliable.

It’s no surprise that one of the first pieces that enterprises focus on first is setting up security policies and guardrails. And on the heels of that comes cultural support for the trust, empowerment, and enablement for the users.

If you can build that foundation of trust as your bedrock before you start changing the world with AI, even if you have some missteps, you’ll have solid ground to support your journey.
