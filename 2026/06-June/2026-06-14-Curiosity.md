My marker board quote for the week. Curiosity is part of the human condition. It’s what drives us to discover, investigate, and research.

Without encouragement, it’s easy in our adult lives to just go through the motions, follow the same path, do the same job.

But with positive encouragement to learn and grow? Our curiosity is how we invent, create, and innovate.

With AI, the landscape changes daily. If you’re not paying attention and learning, you could miss out on being part of transformational journeys that could change the world.
