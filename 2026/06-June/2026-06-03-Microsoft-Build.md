MS Build yesterday, wow! Solara, Majorana, and MAI announced at Satya’s keynote and much more, including the 2026 Cup winners.

Solara - they showed off a wearable AI powered work badge (I’ve lost so many work badges I might be worried about this one) and a physical desktop AI companion

Majorana - Quantum computing? I can’t pretend I’m a physics genius, but the speed and capabilities these devices promise could really change the game as AI moves into that always on and everywhere space.

MAI - can’t wait to try out this new model and see how it ranks up against the competition. Good to have these tech companies all pushing for faster better AI.

I still remember being a kid and my friend got a 20 MB hard drive. We were so excited that it could hold the game that we wanted to play and we figured we would never fill it up.

I never could have dreamed back then that we would see these kinds of technical advancements in my lifetime.

I love that they start the keynote off with the 2026 cup winners. Helping support innovation and genius beyond the walls of Microsoft with cool projects like CopyFlag, SpoilSafe, and Revorah Health.
