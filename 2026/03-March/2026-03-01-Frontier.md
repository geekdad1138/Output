Microsoft has been using the term “Frontier Firm” since last year, and has defined it as:
“A company powered by intelligence on tap, human-agent teams, and a new role for everyone: agent boss.”

They have a good article about it on their Worklab site: 2025: The year the Frontier Firm is born.

It lays out the how they see company AI transformations playing out:

Phase 1: Human with assistant
Phase 2: Human-agent teams
Phase 3: Human-led, agent-operated

It’s exciting to be in the industry while things like this are possible!
