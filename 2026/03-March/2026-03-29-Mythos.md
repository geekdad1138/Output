Anthropic confirmed that Claude Mythos is on it's way, and is a "step change" above Opus, particularly in the Cybersecurity space.

Meaning in short, that it can very quickly find security vulnerabilities, for both the good guys and the bad guys. If you're not using it and the bad guys are, you're at risk.

Jacob Krell put it perfectly in his post about Mythos and Cybersecurity:
"Organizations that restrict their security teams from accessing frontier AI capabilities do not eliminate the offensive use case. They forfeit the defensive one."
