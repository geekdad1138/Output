I can’t tell if Steve Yegge’s gastown is genius or insanity.. or both. But it’s absolutely fascinating and intriguing.

As he put it in his blog a few months ago.. 
“It’s like a late 1800s factory with machines that can disembowel you if you’re not careful.”

It’s beautiful, it’s chaotic, it’s exciting. I can’t wait to see where it ends up. (and I haven’t even mentioned wasteland). It feels like the future we’ll all get to, if we have the courage (and tokens) to take the leap.
