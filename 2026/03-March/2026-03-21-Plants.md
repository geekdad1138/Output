Everybody knows about LLMs right? But what about LPMs.. Large “Plant” Models. Not like manufacturing plants, like living plants.

Apparently they built out a dataset of 150 million plants, and then they use AI to recognize plants and lasers to zap the weeds. No pesticides, just AI.

So what’s next the Large Model someone is going to build?
