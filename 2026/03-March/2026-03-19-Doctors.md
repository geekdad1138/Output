Are doctors using AI, and do they like it?

The American Medical Association published the results of a survey of 1,692 doctors where they answered questions about their use and opinions of AI.

Survey results showed that overall doctors are using it, and see it as a good thing.
"Document-focused applications" were the biggest win for doctors, using AI to help write up all of the paperwork, documentation, and even help with translation services.
