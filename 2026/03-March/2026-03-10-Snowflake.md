Snowflake/Omdia just released a report on Gen AI and Agents, surveying over 2,000 corporations.

It’s got a lot of good detail broken down by usage for different parts of the organizations (IT, finance, etc.) as well as adoption and usage by industry (manufacturing, tech, etc.)

Early adopters of Gen AI and Agents are the ones seeing the biggest gains. From the article:
“92% of early adopters say they’ve seen a positive return on their gen AI investments.”

I think my favorite line was from the opening of the report talking about the world’s mixed feelings about AI:
“We’re told that LLMs can’t count the Rs in strawberry, so they’re useless. At the same time, they’re so smart that they’re going to take all our jobs.”
