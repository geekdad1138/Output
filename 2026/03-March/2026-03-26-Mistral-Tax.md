Should the AI companies pay a tax for the usage of the data they’re trained on?

Arthur Mensch from Mistral had a chat with Financial times about this idea.

From the article: “At Mistral, we are proposing a revenue-based levy that would be applied to all commercial providers placing AI models on the market or putting them into service in Europe, reflecting their use of content publicly available online”
