Google announced at Cloud Next that they’re launching Wiz’s Red/Blue/Green AI Application Protection Program.

If you haven’t heard of the agents and roles by colors it’s pretty cool. Each one has a role whether it’s scanning the environment, determining the impact, or actually remediating the fix.

From their site:
“Our north star is simple:
Investigate and remediate every validated risk at the speed of AI.”
