Claude Design looks amaaaaaazing. I'm a coder by trade not a designer, so the visuals in the demo video for this tool look like a dream to me. I don't have the skills to put together those kinds of designs, so having an AI tool that can not only visualize them but even hand them off to Claude Code to build them?

We're getting closer and closer to the world where we just describe the interfaces and designs we want, and the AI just makes it for us. Coming from a past where I used to program every pixel, color and beep for an animation, to hand keying HTML and scripts to create visual interfaces.. I feel like this has always been the end game.

Even the CEO from Canva Melanie Perkins is optimistic about building on top of the tool:
"We're excited to build on our collaboration with Claude, making it seamless for people to bring ideas and drafts from Claude Design into Canva"
