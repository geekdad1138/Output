Lenovo just released a report showing many companies are suffering from uncontrolled shadow AI, causing “delayed ROI, duplicated spend, increased attack surface, and lack of visibility.”

While some workers were having successes with corporate approved AI, others found ways to use AI outside of the company’s control. Either because they lacked training or availability to AI tools. They surveyed 6,000 workers across multiple enterprise companies to compile the data.

The full report from Lenovo promotes a way to address these problems using their AI transformation playbook, which outlines steps for: “what leaders must do guided adoption and funnel it into high-value transformation”
A four step plan:
1. Unite, 2. Coach, 3. Build, 4. Triumph.
