A new ethics study from Harvard shows AIs acting unethically to maximize profits at any cost.

This shouldn’t be a surprise to anyone, the LLMs are trained on massive amounts of data. This includes shady business practices, that the AI can copy without constraints and barriers.

With the rise of things like aCommerce, we’re going to need to have well defined laws, guardrails and auditing.

tl;dr “they learned it from watching us”
