Agentic Transformation and the Agentic Enteprise, two big topics coming out of Google’s Cloud Next ‘26.

The idea is that we are past the time where companies should be deciding IF they are going to implement AI. They should now be deciding HOW to transform their company with AI agentic technology. Google has developed their Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform, and launched a 750 million dollar partner fund to get everyone on board.

So much news lately has been about Claude, NVIDIA, and Copilot. But you can be sure that Google isn’t going to be left out of all the AI Agentic fun.
