Product Operating Models are the key to a successful AI investment, instead of traditional project operating models. 

That’s part of the message from Leah T. Brown’s article over at IT Revolution, and as always the content from that group is top notch.

The article calls in the results of the planview 2024 state of the industry, that found the companies that measured on customer adoption, revenue impact, and business result performed better than ones that measured on deadlines, budgets and scopes.

From the article:
“Project Management is a powerful tool, but it was designed for a different kind of problem. Applying it to digital product development is like using a construction schedule to manage a market.”
