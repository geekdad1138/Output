The 5Cs of AI: Curiousity, Courage, Creativity, Compassion, Communication

On Microsoft's worklab site, they just posted a great article about a new book about AI, written by Ryan Roslansky and Aneesh Raman.

The book is called "Open to Work: How to Get Ahead in the Age of AI" and it's about these 5Cs that we need to develop to work better with AI.

From the article: "the 5Cs aren’t capabilities you can pick up from a weekend workshop or an online training module. They are core pieces of who we are and how we think as humans, and they develop only through time, connection, and challenge."
