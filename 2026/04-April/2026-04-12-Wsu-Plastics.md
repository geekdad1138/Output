Fancy cameras plus AI to improve our plastic recycling? Yes!

We spend a lot of time talking about how AI is going to write code better or make us all a bajillion dollars.

Meanwhile PhD student Maria Paula Garcia Tovar at Washington state is using hyperspectral imaging with CNNs to help improve recycling.

From the article:
“the research team sought to investigate the feasibility of plastic identification in a simulation using hyperspectral imaging technology and convolutional neural networks (CNNs) — a deep-learning artificial intelligence model often lauded for its ability to process complex image data”

Very cool.
